# Sales Insights Dashboard for UrbanRide Bikes
Data analysis dashboard projects for UrbanRide Bikes, identifying customer segments, sales behavior and generate actionable customer insights.

## Problem Statement
UrbanRide Bikes lacks clarity on which customer segment are the most likely to purchase bicycles. Without evidence based data, promotions are broad, and inaccurate target market, this will result in lower conversion rates and higher acquisition costs.

The available customer dataset include demographics, income, commute behavior and purchase history but the dataset was not yet integrated into an actionable plan.

## Business Objective
The key characteristics of customers who are most likely to buy a bike and translate the findings into recommendations that improve:
* Marketing targeting
* Conversion rates
* Campaign ROI
* Customer segmentation strategy

## My Approach
1. Examine and observe data
2. Cleaned and standardized the raw Excel dataset
3. Validated missing/duplicate records
4. Performed exploratory data analysis
5. Create pivot table to compare purchase behavior across demographics
6. Build an interactive Excel dashboard to visualize
7. Gather findings into business recommendations

## Tools & Techniques Used
* Microsoft Excel
    * Data cleaning & transformation
    * Pivot Tables
    * Slicer
    * Interactive dashboard design
* Data Analysis
* Segmentation Analysis
* Descriptive Statistics

## Key Insights

##### Overall
* 48% of customers purchased a bike

##### Demographics
* Customers aged 30-40 years show the highest purchase rate oif 61%
* Customers after age 60 declines the purchase of bikes

##### Income
* Higher income groups purchase more frequently
* Customer earning $80K show the strongest conversion of 51-53%

##### Commute Distance
* 0-1 miles have the highest purchase rate
* Long commutes (10+ miles) has the lowest purchase of 29%

##### Behavioral
* Customer with fewer cars and shorter commutes tend to buy bikes more often which indicates that bikes are used for short-distance mobility, or lifestyle

## Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, marketing efforts should prioritize high-probability customer segments, particularly customers aged 30-40, earning $80K or more, and customers with commute distances under 5 miles as these group show the highest purchase rates. Campaign should focus on urban and suburban areas where shorter commute patterns which makes bicycles a transportation option. The brand should emphasize convenience, health benefits and bikes as efficient transportation for nearby travel. To improve ROI, marketing should be reduce for lower-conversion segments such as long-distance commuters (10+ miles) and older age groups. For future improvements, the company can enhance decision-making by developing predictive models such as classification algorithms, conducting A/B tests on targeted campaigns and incorportating seasonality and pricing to uncover deeper insights.
